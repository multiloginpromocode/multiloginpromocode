Promo Verification: Run a Small Proof Before You Redeem

Coupons look great on listicles — prove eligibility and governance before committing budget.

Updated: 2026-04-12

3-Step Promo Proof

  1. Confirm coupon code validity at official checkout.
  2. Run a tiny team pilot to validate roles and billing behavior.
  3. Record evidence and lock-in only after governance checks pass.

Safer Checkout Pattern

Don’t approve annual spend during an initial test. Confirm workspace roles, billing owner, and exit paths first.
